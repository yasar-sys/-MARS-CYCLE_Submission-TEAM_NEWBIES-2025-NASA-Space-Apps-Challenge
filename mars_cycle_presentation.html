🌟 Key Innovation

Carbon Utilization Hub

MARS-CYCLE uniquely transforms carbon extracted from Mars' CO₂ atmosphere into high-value products including water filters, air purification systems, and structural composites.

This creates a dual-purpose system that supports both atmospheric processing and manufacturing—truly living off the land on Mars!

🛡️ Safety & Sustainability

Safety Features

  • ✅ No Combustion - Sealed thermal processes only
  • ✅ Zero Toxic Emissions - HEPA filtration throughout
  • ✅ No PFAS Generated - Careful material selection
  • ✅ Microplastic Prevention - All wastewater filtered
  • ✅ Emergency Shutdown - Automatic system isolation

Sustainability

  • 🌱 Closed-Loop - Minimal external inputs
  • 🌱 Regolith Integration - Uses local Martian soil
  • 🌱 Solar Powered - Each station has panels
  • 🌱 Scalable - Modular design for growth
  • 🌱 10+ Year Lifespan - Long-term reliability
